I am **@johnjaejunlee95**, a 5th-year Ph.D. candidate at the **Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence, UNIST**. My research stems from a fundamental question: **"How do AI models learn and generalize?"** * **Past:** Meta-Learning, Generalization * **Current:** Multimodal Learning, Robustness, Biomedical AI In the early stages of my research, I focused primarily on **meta-learning**. More recently, my work has centered on **multimodal learning**, where I investigate its theoretical foundations and interpret its behavior from the perspectives of **robustness** and **generalization**. I have also developed a growing interest in **bioinformatics** and **biosignal analysis**. My goal extends beyond developing models that simply achieve strong performance. Instead, I aspire to become a researcher who understands and explains AI through **solid theoretical and mathematical foundations**. I am particularly interested in uncovering the underlying principles behind how AI models learn and operate—both intuitively and theoretically—and in communicating complex engineering concepts in a clear, accessible, and engaging way.
